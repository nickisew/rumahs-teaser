About Rumahs

Rumah means house or home in Indonesian. It’s a place to make new friends from all over the world who are looking to share homes and plan remote adventures together.

It will give groups the autonomy to find rentals together and split payments securely without sending money to friends they haven't met yet ;) Rumahs will help faciliate the payment and the return of funds if any one in the group changes their mind before the rental is booked.
