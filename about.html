Our Mission

WWe're building a platform that allows digital nomads to connect and share rentals around the world. Our vision is to create a community for making meaningful connections while traveling. Whether you’re an introvert or an extrovert, a surfer or a history buff, it’s a place to find a group with whom you’d want to share a cozier (or more luxurious) accommodation on your next remote adventure.

Why 'Rumahs'?

The idea for Rumahs was born during solo traveling through South East Asia. Exploring can be lonely as a solo traveler. Often, the most affordable option to stay somewhere social is in hostels or co-working spaces, but it's always a gamble who you'll meet there. Sometimes, there’s groups who aren't interested in meeting new people. Other times, you click with someone, but your trips only overlapped by a day or two. And while it’s easy to meet plenty of outgoing, party-focused travelers, it’s nice to connect with people you can kick back with.

What I really wanted was a home - where I could relax outside my room and cook my own meals with good company. And I didn’t want it curated by an agency or a surf camp. I wanted to make my own friend group with people from all over the world.

How cool would it be to share a home with other travelers I get along with? But where do I meet them? And how do we agree on a rental, book it and safely split the cost? Meeting people online is easy, but the logistics of renting a place together isn't.

That's what Rumahs is for.

It’s a place to connect with like-minded people and book rentals as a group - no matter where you are in the world when you meet.

As we build the beta, we want your input. If the idea excites you, join the waitlist. And if something would hold you back from using Rumahs, just reply to the confirmation email because we'd love to hear it.

We hope you’ll be on Rumahs making new friends from all over the world soon!
