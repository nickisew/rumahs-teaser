Our Mission

We're building a platform that allows digital nomads to connect and share beautiful homes around the world. Our vision is to create a community where strangers can meet online, safely share accommodations, and create meaningful connections while traveling solo.

Why 'Rumahs'?

The idea for Rumahs was born during my solo travels through South East Asia last year. I loved exploring, but it could be lonely. As a solo traveler, the most affordable option is usually hostels or coworking apartments, but it's always a gamble who you'll meet. Sometimes, there's groups who aren't interested in meeting new people. Other times, you click with someone, but your trips only overlapped by a day or two. And while I met plenty of really fun outgoing, party-focused travelers, I wanted to connect with people I could kick back with.

What I really wanted was a home - where I could relax outside my room and cook my own meals with good company.

That's when I realized how cool it would be to share a home with other like-minded travelers. But then came the questions of where do I find them, how do we agree on a place, and how do we safely split the cost? Meeting people online is easy, but figuring out the logistics of renting a place together isn't.

That's what Rumahs is for. Rumah means "house" or "home" in Indonesian.

We're building a platform that makes it simple for digital nomads and other travelers to connect, find homes together, and confidently share the cost.

As we build the beta, we want your input. If the idea excites you, join the waitlist. And if something would hold you back from using Rumahs, just reply to the confirmation email because we'd love to hear it.

We hope you’ll be on Rumahs making new friends from all over the world soon!
